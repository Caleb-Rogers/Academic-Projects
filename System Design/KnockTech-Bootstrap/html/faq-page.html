Who is KnockTech?
KnockTech is our revolutionary virtually-guided freestanding heavy bag. Unlike a typical punching bag, our product introduces technology that tracks your movement and hits, and uses the resulting data to formulate a routine that will provide smart workouts, gratifying routines, and entertaining boxing scenarios that combines your love of martial arts with the advanced world of technology today.

What are its uses?
KnockTech can be connected anywhere from your laptop, your computer, your SmartTV, and even your phone! Audio will be provided from the base of the heavy bag, but the optimal use of this product is combining it with our developed connections to a nearby smart TV, phone, or computer.
